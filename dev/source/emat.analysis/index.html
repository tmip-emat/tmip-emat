Scenario Discovery¶

Scenario discovery in exploratory modeling is focused on finding scenarios that are interesting to the user. The process generally begins through the identification of particular outcomes that are “of interest”, and then discovering what factor or combination of factors can result in those outcomes. There are a variety of methods to use for scenario discovery. We illustrate a few here.

Directed Search¶

The scenario discovery tools outlined above are focused on exploring parameters and outcomes across a pre-set sample of model runs. This sample can be small or quite large, but the tools only consider cases that have already been evaluated. In directed search, we transition to tools that will propose and execute new cases.
